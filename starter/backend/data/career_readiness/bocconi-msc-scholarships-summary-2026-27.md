# Bocconi MSc scholarships and tuition waivers — summary 2026-27

This note consolidates the merit and need-based funding options available to Master of Science (MSc) applicants at Bocconi University for the 2026-27 academic year.

## Bocconi Graduate Merit Award

The Bocconi Graduate Merit Award is the university's flagship merit-based scholarship for MSc students. **The award consists of a full tuition and fees waiver only (no stipend).** It covers 100% of Bocconi's ordinary academic tuition and fees, with the value approximately **EUR 13,000-EUR 14,000 per year** depending on the program. A limited number of awardees may also receive free accommodation in a Bocconi Residence.

The award is automatically considered as part of the MSc admission application — no separate scholarship application is required. Awards are assigned based on academic merit and the strength of the full admission dossier.

The Merit Award is renewable for the second year of the MSc, provided the awardee earns at least 50 credits by 10 August of year one. The award is tied to the program and admission round in which it was originally granted; it cannot be transferred to a different program if the student requests a change.

## Bocconi International Award

The Bocconi International Award is a separate merit-based scholarship offering a **50% tuition reduction** for both Bachelor and MSc students. Eligibility and selection follow the same admissions-based evaluation as the Merit Award.

## Women Awards (STEM MSc)

Since AY 2021-2022 Bocconi has offered "Women Awards", a special type of Bocconi Graduate Merit Award reserved for female students enrolled in STEM MSc programs. Format and renewal terms match the standard Merit Award (full tuition waiver, 50-credit renewal threshold).

## Cyber Partnership Scholarship

Students admitted to the MSc in Cyber Risk Strategy and Governance (Joint Degree with Politecnico di Milano) may receive the Cyber Partnership Scholarship, which is **equivalent to a Bocconi Graduate Merit Award** in value and terms.

## Bocconi4Access to Education

A separate need-based scheme for first-year MSc students that may include 100% tuition waivers. It requires a separate application via the "Bocconi4Access to Education" online procedure within the published deadlines.

## ISU Bocconi Scholarship

The ISU Bocconi Scholarship is the university's main need-based scholarship system, available to undergraduate and graduate students whose family income falls below specified thresholds. Awards combine tuition discounts, housing benefits, and meal credits according to bracket.
