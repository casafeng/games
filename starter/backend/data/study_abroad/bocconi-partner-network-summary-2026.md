# Bocconi partner university network — summary

This note consolidates Bocconi's international partner-school network figures from the official Bocconi Exchange Program and International Networks pages.

## Exchange Program network

How many partner universities does Bocconi have across how many countries? Bocconi maintains agreements with **293 partner universities in 56 countries across five continents** for its Exchange Program. The Exchange Program offers approximately **2,100 slots in total per academic year**, of which about **650 are at the graduate (MSc) level**. Students attend a study term at the host institution without paying tuition fees there; they cover their own travel, accommodation, and living costs.

## Joint and Double Degree network

How many Double Degree partner schools does Bocconi have? Beyond the Exchange Program, Bocconi has **37 Joint and Double Degree partner schools in 20 countries**. Double Degree students spend one year at Bocconi and one year at a partner institution and earn both a Bocconi *Laurea Magistrale* and the partner's degree.

## Mobility participation

What percent of Bocconi MSc students spend time abroad? More than **50% of Bocconi MSc students** spend a semester or full academic year abroad through Exchange, Double Degree, free-mover, or summer-school programs.

## Continental coverage

The Exchange Program network spans **five continents**: Europe, the Americas, Asia, Oceania, and Africa.
